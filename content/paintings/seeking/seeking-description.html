Seeking

acrylic on canvas, 140x100cm (55"x39")

We are seeking for many things in life - attention, love, acceptance for starters. We seek new experiences, new tastes, new fragrances. It is imprinted into a human nature as it is a natural consequence of our curiosity, needs, desires. "Skeeking" is a painting about our needs and desires within this particular moment. It is painting that is ment to be a psychological excercie, a mindfulness traning to ask ourselves - What am I seeking?

  • Exhibited at solo exhibition "Not obvious forms" at Museum of Krajeńska Land, Nakło nad Notecią, Januray 2023
