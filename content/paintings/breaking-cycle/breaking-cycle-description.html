Breaking cycle

acrylic on raw linen, 70x70cm (27.5591"x27.5591")

Cycles are a natural part of our everydaylife. From the seasons to choosing our life partners - it is happening in some kind of a pattern. Some patterns are having minor effect on our lives while others tend to have a massive and destructive effects, yet we hold on to them. Breaking cycle is an invitation to change. To break free.
