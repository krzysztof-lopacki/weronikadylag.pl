Self awareness

acrylic on canvas, 100x100cm (39"x39")

Self awareness - what is it? When do we say that somebody is aware of self? If I have unconcius motives for my behavior that I am not aware of can I say I am self aware? Is simply noticing things that are happening to us enough to say that we are self-aware? Does simply feeling makes us self-aware? Dubito ergo cogit, cogito ergo sum - was Descartes right? Dubito?
