Attentive

acrylic on canvas, 100x120cm (39"x47")

"Paying close attention to something" - Are you? Are you really paying attention to: what you see? What do you hear? to your kids? to your partner? to your own feelings? to your body? to your emotions? Do you really pay attention?

Attentive is a painting-reminder and like seeking it is a psychological exercise - a mindfulness training of paying attention. The painting was created in many sessions, layer by layer. In it's form it alludes to organic forms and was a part of "Not obvious forms" solo exhibition at Museum of Krajenska Land.

  • Exhibited at solo exhibition "Not obvious forms" at Museum of Krajeńska Land, Nakło nad Notecią, Poland, Januray 2023
  • Group exhibition Thomson Gallery, Zug, Switzerland, January - February 2023
  • Group exhibition Art Lab, Los Aangeles, US, January 2023
