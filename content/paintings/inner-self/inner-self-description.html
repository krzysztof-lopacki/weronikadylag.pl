Inner self

acrylic on canvas, 100x100cm (39"x39")

The work was inspired by the work of Ismael Lagares that was exhibited in Lanford Gallery. I have never thought about using red before. It never spoke to me - it seemed too aggressive and too loud. I never wanted to stand out and red is a colour that makes you pop out so it was natural for me to avoid it. But Ismael Lagares' work showed me that red can be also joyful, playful if you like.So I started exploring and "Inner self" is one of the revelations I had about red and about myself - it's ok to stand out. As Paul Klein once said: "Be distinctive"

  • Group exhibition Thomson Gallery, Zug, Switzerland, January - February 2023
