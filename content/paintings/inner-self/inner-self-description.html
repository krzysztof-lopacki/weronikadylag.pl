Inner self

acrylic on canvas, 100x100cm (39"x39")

Self just like awareness is one of the terms we just accepted but never been able to determin and describe properly. Who we are inside, who/what dives us, who exactly has desires is a matter of study. What part of the brain is responsible for self?

  • Exhibited at solo exhibition "Not obvious forms" at Museum of Krajeńska Land, Nakło nad Notecią, Poland, Januray 2023
  • Group exhibition Thomson Gallery, Zug, Switzerland, January - February 2023
  • Group exhibition Art Lab, Los Aangeles, US, January 2023
