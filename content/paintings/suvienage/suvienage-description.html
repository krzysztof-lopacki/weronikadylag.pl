Suvienage

acrylic on primed linen canvas, 100x170cm (39"x67")

Sometimes we are so focused on our day to day tasks, we are forgetting that we are more than our work, more than our societal roles.

Suvinage, the inaugural piece of my new opus, "Journey". This neologism, Suvinage, a construct of my own imagination, has yet to find a place in any vernacular. It is an emblem of my singular mind, much like the painting itself, which was inspired by the visual journey of Chuang Che's oeuvre.

This term encapsulates the essence of a transformative odyssey, one that requires courage, self-exploration, and personal development. It is an ode to the human spirit's innate desire to embark on a journey of self-discovery and growth. Suvinage invites the viewer to join me on this expedition, to embrace the unknown, and to discover what lies beyond the horizon.
